Who we are

Cadright is a practice–driven collective working at the intersection of technology, research and the built environment. Our network brings together professionals from diverse backgrounds to solve real-world problems with data, design and digital tools.

We design, deploy and manage research-backed frameworks for clients ranging from universities and schools to private ventures and industry partners. Our goal is simple: turn complex challenges into clear, actionable solutions.
