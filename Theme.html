RESEARCH THEMES

What we research at CGRC

Our research is structured into interconnected themes that guide our projects, publications, and collaborations.

Energy-efficient & Climate-responsive Buildings

We explore design strategies, performance optimisation techniques, and adaptive solutions tailored to African climates.

Green Skills, Jobs & Workforce Development

Mapping skills gaps, future job demands, and pathways for building a resilient African green workforce.

Education, Curriculum & Professional Development

Research into sustainable education frameworks, curriculum innovation, and professional training programmes.

Policy, Practice & Implementation Research

Analysing policies and implementation challenges to improve green practices at scale across African cities.
